# FL Studio Shortcuts

## Transport
- `Space`: Play/Pause
- `Ctrl+Space`: Stop
- `R`: Record
- `Ctrl+R`: Record Audio
- `L`: Loop Recording

## View
- `F5`: Playlist
- `F6`: Channel Rack
- `F7`: Piano Roll
- `F8`: Browser
- `F9`: Mixer

## Tools
- `C`: Cut/Split
- `D`: Delete
- `B`: Paint/Brush
- `P`: Paint Selection
- `M`: Mute/Unmute

## Piano Roll
- `Alt+Z`: Zoom In
- `Alt+X`: Zoom Out
- `Alt+C`: Zoom Default
- `Q`: Quick Quantize
- `Shift+Q`: Quantize Settings
