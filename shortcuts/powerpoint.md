# Microsoft PowerPoint Shortcuts

## Slide Management
- `Ctrl+M`: New Slide
- `Ctrl+D`: Duplicate Slide
- `Ctrl+Shift+>`: Increase Font
- `Ctrl+Shift+<`: Decrease Font
- `F5`: Start Presentation

## Editing
- `Ctrl+X`: Cut
- `Ctrl+C`: Copy
- `Ctrl+V`: Paste
- `Ctrl+Z`: Undo
- `Ctrl+Y`: Redo

## Text Formatting
- `Ctrl+B`: Bold
- `Ctrl+I`: Italic
- `Ctrl+U`: Underline
- `Ctrl+L`: Left Align
- `Ctrl+E`: Center Align

## Presentation Mode
- `N`: Next Slide
- `P`: Previous Slide
- `B`: Black Screen
- `W`: White Screen
- `Esc`: End Show
